Jobs of Submarine pilots

Submarine pilots are people who control ROVs (Remotely Operating Vehicles) to take evidence for researching purposes such as:
  • Scientific Research
  • Mapping Ocean Floor
  • Performing Underwater Construction / Salvage

Difficulties faced by Submarine Pilots controling ROVs

  • Low Visibility
  • Unpredictable Currents
  • Leaks
  • High Pressure
  • Technical Issues
  • Physical Demands (Physical strain of operating in confined spaces)

Solution for submarine pilots


Due to low visibility, the Pilot faces trouble, so the submarine can have implemented SONAR(slightly harmful), Radar, and Night Vision. For unpredictable currents, leaks, high pressure, we could make the body of the submarine (heavy steel device) thicker using Titanium Alloy (Strong yet light material). Technical issues could happen at any time, so much precaution could not be taken, yet we could make sure the wires and electric devices are properly insulated and waterproofed. Now due to confined space, we could make the body slimmer and make the front side into a streamlined body to help, in speed. This submarine should have an imaging system to help capture pictures of the ocean for mapping. By adding Manipulator arms the pilot can perfom collection of samples, construction and valve turns.
